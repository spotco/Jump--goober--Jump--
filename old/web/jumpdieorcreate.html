Quick writeup about level editor since there is no ingame help yet. Bottom left corner is the picker for what kind of block you want to create. Select your type and click and drag on the main screen. Z or click undo to delete. To make text, click the T box, click anywhere on screen, enter you text and press ok. The bottom 20 pixels of the screen will not try to make a textbox when you click on them, click there to get keyboard focus on the game (same bug mentioned above). Boxes must be at least 7pixels wide+think or they don't register. To submit a level, beat it.This will bring up a window option and just follow the instructions.
